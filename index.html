Gen Z Glossary

Full glossary →

Political terms, explained in plain language. No spin.

Budget Deficit
When government spends more than it collects in taxes. Kenya's 2025 deficit was KSh 831 billion — borrowed money funded essential services.
Economy · Finance
EACC
Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission. Can investigate but cannot prosecute — referral to the DPP is required. Prosecutions remain rare since 2011.
Institutions · Corruption
National Debt
Money the government owes locally and abroad. At KSh 11.1 trillion, every Kenyan effectively owes ~KSh 207,000.
Economy · Finance
Gazette Notice
Published in the Kenya Gazette — the official government record. A decision only becomes legally binding once gazetted.
Law · Government
Vote of No Confidence
A parliamentary motion to remove a leader. Requires a majority. Rarely succeeds due to party discipline.
Parliament · Politics
Supplementary Budget
An additional mid-year budget when spending diverges from the original plan. Kenya has passed one every fiscal year for 5 consecutive years.
Budget · Parliament
